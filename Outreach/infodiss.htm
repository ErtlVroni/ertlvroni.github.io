Information workshop concerning PhD positions


Are you thinking about doing a PhD after your Masters?
Then you might find the workshop helpful that we are organising for master students of our department. We invited speakers with different backgrounds, from different countries who will not only talk about their experience of doing a PhD, but also give the participants useful information on application procedures and programs in different countries.
