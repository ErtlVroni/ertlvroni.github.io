Approximate outline of the class

  • 26th August: Functions, graphs, slope of line.
  • 28th August: Introduction to limits, definition.
  • 4th September: Limit properties, theorems.
  • 9th September: Trigonometric limits.
  • 11th September: Limits at infinity.
  • 16th September: Continuity.
  • 18th September: Review.
  • 23rd September: First Midterm.
  • 25th September: Introduction/Definition of derivatives.
  • 30th September: Differentiation rules.
  • 2nd October: Derivatives of trigonometric functions. Chain rule.
  • 7th October: Higher order derivatives..
  • 9th October: Implicit differentiation.
  • 21st October: Related rates.
  • 23rd October: Review.
  • 28th October: Second Midterm.
  • 30th October: Maximum and Minimum Values.
  • 4th Novermber: Monotonicity, concavity.
  • 6th November: Local Extrema, Graphing.
  • 11th Novemebr: Mean Value Theorem. Practical Problems.
  • 13th Novemebr: Review.
  • 18th November: Third Midterm.
  • 20th November: Introduction to Area.
  • 25th November: The Definite and Indefinite Integral.
  • 27th November: First Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
  • 2nd December: Second Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
  • 4th Decemebr: Mean Value Theorem for Integrals.
  • 9th December: Review.
  • 11th December: Solutions for the practice exam.
