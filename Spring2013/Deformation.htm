Arithmetic Geometry Seminar Spring 2013

p-adic Deformation of Algebraic Cycles

(after S. Bloch, H. Esnault, M. Kerz)

This semester we are organising a seminar on p-adic deformation of algebraic cycle classes. This is open to graduate students or anyone with background in arithmetic geometry.
We will meet in JFB B-1. The first meeting is on Wedenesday 23rd January 2013 at 13:00, and every Wednesday after that starting at the same time. This may be subject to change which will be posted here.
The goal is to study the article p-adic deformation of algebraic cycle classes by Spencer Bloch, Hélène Esnault and Moritz Kerz.
