Project

Students will have one project to turn in. The project will be due 30th November 2010. The list of topics will appear on the course home page on 19th October 2010. All the necessary information will be given in class and online. Students will work in groups of four or five on a topic that the group selects. Late projects are not accepted.

  • Organising the Projects:
    Form groups of 3-4 students till 19th October.
    Projects of groups with less than 2 students will generally not be accepted.
    Notify me by 21st October about your group.
    You will have one hour in class to discuss the project and to form groups.
